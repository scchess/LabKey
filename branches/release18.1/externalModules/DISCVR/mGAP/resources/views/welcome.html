About mGAP

The Macaque Genotype and Phenotype Database (mGAP) provides access to genotype data collected on a large, pedigreed, rhesus macaque colony housed at the Oregon National Primate Research Center (ONPRC). We hope to provide researchers with the tools to explore naturally occurring genetic variation in macaques. Our database of DNA variants is curated and heavily annotated, and can either be viewed through our genome browser or downloaded for external use.

To request an account, please use the 'Request Access' link at the bottom of this page. mGAP is an NIH funded project, supported by R24OD021324.
